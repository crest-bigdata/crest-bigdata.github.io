Infrastructures & Analytics

"Empowering the future of the data."

We investigate, develop, and evaluate big data systems for real-time collection, storage, processing, and visualization of large-scale data.

We study, implement, and evaluate various big data analytical frameworks (e.g., Hadoop, Spark, and Flink) and associated technologies for optimal use in various application domains.

We design and implement platform that offers big data analytics-as-a-service to automate infrastructure, selection and usage of big data technologies for users lacking big data expertise and resources.
